DESERVED Trust

Community
Guidelines.

DESERVED is built for real Calgary people, useful connections, and respectful opportunity. These simple rules help keep the platform clean and trustworthy.

Be real.

Use honest information. Do not create fake profiles, fake skills, fake opportunities, fake stories, or misleading claims.

Be respectful.

No harassment, hate, threats, discrimination, spam, scams, pressure tactics, or inappropriate messages. Contact people only for relevant and respectful reasons.

Be useful.

DESERVED is not for random posting or attention seeking. Share what you can do, what you need, what you offer, and how someone can connect with purpose.

Respect privacy.

Do not share someone else's private contact details, screenshots, messages, address, workplace, or personal information without permission.

Admin review matters.

Profiles, reports, and stories may be reviewed by the DESERVED admin team. We may reject, hide, or remove profiles that appear unsafe, fake, disrespectful, incomplete, or not aligned with the platform.

Privacy & Safety

Connect carefully.
Stay responsible.

DESERVED helps people discover and connect. It does not replace your own judgment, safety checks, or responsibility when you communicate, meet, hire, collaborate, or share information.

What DESERVED does

  • Provides a platform for people to create profiles and be discovered.
  • Uses admin review to reduce spam, fake profiles, and low-quality submissions.
  • Uses double opt-in connection requests before contact information is shared.
  • Allows users to report concerns or issues.

What DESERVED does not do

  • We do not run background checks.
  • We do not verify criminal history, employment history, certifications, identity documents, or financial claims.
  • We do not guarantee someone’s services, skills, behaviour, availability, safety, or outcomes.
  • We do not supervise private conversations, meetings, payments, jobs, projects, or collaborations.

Your responsibility

Share personal information carefully. Meet in safe public places when appropriate. Verify important claims yourself. Use written agreements for paid work. Do not send money or sensitive information unless you fully trust the situation.

Need to report something? Use the Contact / Report Issue page and include the profile name, what happened, and any useful details. We will review it as soon as possible.

Simple Terms

Terms of
Use.

These simple terms explain the basic understanding for using DESERVED. They are written clearly for early-stage use and can be expanded later as the platform grows.

1. Platform purpose

DESERVED is a community connection platform. It helps people share profiles, skills, stories, needs, offers, and connection requests.

2. No guarantees

DESERVED does not guarantee jobs, clients, income, collaborations, mentorship, services, safety, or results. Any connection or opportunity depends on the users involved.

3. User responsibility

You are responsible for what you post, who you contact, what information you share, and any private decisions you make after connecting.

4. Admin rights

DESERVED may review, approve, reject, hide, or remove profiles, stories, reports, and content that appears unsafe, fake, spammy, incomplete, disrespectful, or harmful to the community.

5. Early platform notice

DESERVED is currently an early Calgary-first platform. Features, policies, pages, and rules may be updated as the platform grows.
